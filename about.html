Our Story

ATLABEST started as a sole business in 2016. It first provide its services in only areas around Makerere upto 2018 when it maximized market.We started by supplying electronic accessories to individual buyers, and joined the bidding system to find access of supplying companies and institutions with different electrical gadgets.

Every program was designed and implemented from our home where we stayed in Makerere Kikoni, until 2017 when we formed our headquaters at Senate Buildings which is admistration block located inside Makerere University, and openning up other branches in different areas of Uganda and East Africa at large.
